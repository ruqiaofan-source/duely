Settle football bets with your mates — on the record.

Duely is the free app to settle a bet with a friend. Call the match, your mate takes the other side, and when it finishes the winner goes on the record. You get a shareable challenge card, a running rivalry table, and season-long bragging rights. Duely holds no money — you settle any stake between yourselves. 18+.

How it works

  1. Call it. Pick a match and back your outcome — who wins, a draw, or your own custom call.
  2. Send the link. Share a challenge link on WhatsApp; your mate taps it and takes the other side.
  3. Settle it. After full time you both confirm the result. The winner goes on the record and the rivalry table updates.
